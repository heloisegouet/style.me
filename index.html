Our Mission

Inspired by the movie Clueless, styleME aims to give every girl the fashion style she deserves. In this inspiring movie scene, the main character uses a system on her computer to help her choose her outfit for the day.

This website helps young girls to feel good in their outfit and still be themselves, to help them be confident in their style.

We live in a society where most girls are dressed the same and yet, we all want to be different.

The idea of the site is also to promote responsible fashion by encouraging people to buy from more environmentally friendly brands. The outfits will therefore be made only from clothes from slow fashion brands.
